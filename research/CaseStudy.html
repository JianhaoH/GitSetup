Discrepancy in diagnosis and treatment of post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD): Treatment for the wrong reason
How is or how might technology be used to improve the effectiveness of the ways people are trying to address the root causes of the problem?

Virtual reality may become a method of therapy.As you expose yourself to triggers through virtual reality, you have the chance to confront your fear in a safe environment. As with traditional prolonged exposure treatment, this allows you to learn how to cope with your anxiety and reframe your thinking about the traumatic event. Advances in technology may also help in the diagnosis of PTSD with technologies such as imaging data, computer or smartphone questionnaires, or biometric data (sleep, HRV, skin conductance) using connected objects. Using MRI scans and ML makes diagonsis of PTSD possible with a 92.5 % accuracy.
